About CHINESE CHRISTIANS FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE

CHINESE CHRISTIANS FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE is organized under California’s Nonprofit Public Benefit Corporation Law and operates exclusively for charitable and educational purposes as a recognized 501(c)(3) with EIN 85-2489966.

Specific purpose: educate and promote Social Justice among the Chinese community as mandated by the Holy Scripture.

  • No substantial part of our activities involves lobbying or political campaigning.
  • Assets are irrevocably dedicated to charitable and educational purposes.
  • Upon dissolution, remaining assets go to a similar 501(c)(3).
