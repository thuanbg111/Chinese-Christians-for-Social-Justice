CHINESE CHRISTIANS FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE

We are a California 501(c)(3) nonprofit public benefit corporation. Our mission is to educate and promote Social Justice among the Chinese community as mandated by the Holy Scripture.

What We Do

Faith‑informed education and community action for justice.

  • Scripture‑based teaching on justice and mercy
  • Community dialogues & service projects
  • Partnerships with churches and nonprofits

Scripture & Justice Academy

Workshops and small‑group studies exploring Social Justice in the Bible and practical discipleship.

Community Outreach

Service days, food/hygiene drives, and referrals to aid providers — serving neighbors with dignity.

Partnerships

Collaborations with churches, campus groups, and nonprofits to multiply impact in Chinese communities.
